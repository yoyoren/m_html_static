沙哈被誉为巧克力糕点的国王。沙哈蛋糕诞生在1832年，由澳大利亚驻法外交官的专任主厨，法国人沙哈所发明的，当年他16岁。因外交官“梅兹提尔尼西”嗜好甜食，每天都必须品尝不同的甜点，沙哈试着将许多平常不常用于点心的食材加以组合，最后完成了这道“沙哈蛋糕”。后来在1876年，沙哈的儿子爱德华创立“沙哈HOTEL”，并与妻子安娜把这道点心加以发扬光大，成为法国著名的蛋糕之一。

1832 and Prince Wenzel von Metternich has an important
assignment. He demands his personal chef create a special
dessert for a group of important guests. As if that weren’t enough pressure, the Prince adds, “Let there be no shame on me tonight.” Unfortunately the chef is taken ill, the task falls to 16 year old apprentice Franz Sacher. The young Sacher created a dense, rich chocolate cake, rippled with layers of apricot jam and iced with dark chocolate. The Sachertorte, as it’s now known, did not bring shame on
the Prince. It’s still enjoyed today and the
secret recipe has been the subject of a
fierce legal battle.
